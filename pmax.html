Setting Up Your Performance Max Campaign

A great campaign starts with a strong setup. Here's how to get it right:

1

Optimize Your Product Feed

For online retailers, your Google Merchant Center feed is the heart of a Performance Max campaign. Upload details like product titles, prices, images, and categories.

Action: Use descriptive, keyword-rich titles and high-resolution images that grab attention.
2

Define Your Campaign Objective

Choose an objective like Sales, Leads, or Store Visits in Google Ads to align Google's AI with your priorities.

3

Set Conversion Goals

Tell Google what "success" means—purchases, form submissions, or phone calls. Accurate data here fuels the AI's optimization engine.

4

Establish Budget and Bidding

Decide your daily budget—aim for at least 3x your target CPA for flexibility. Choose between Maximize Conversions or Maximize Conversion Value.

5

Target Locations and Languages

Select where your ads will show and the languages your audience speaks. Opting for "All languages" can widen your reach to multilingual users.

6

Launch with Confidence

Review your setup—goals, budget, targeting—and click "Publish." Your campaign is now live, ready to adapt and perform across Google's network!

Crafting Ads That Convert

The Role of Assets in Performance Max

Your ads are only as good as the assets you provide. Here's how to make them irresistible:

Key Elements: Build multiple asset groups with text, images, videos and logos. Write compelling headlines and descriptions, add eye-catching visuals, and include engaging videos when possible.
Pro Tip: Aim for "Excellent" Ad Strength by diversifying your asset mix and maintaining high quality across all elements.

Targeting Smarter with Audience Signals

Guide Google's AI to Your Ideal Customers

Audience signals are clues you give Google's AI about your ideal customers—think of them as starting points, not strict filters.

Best Practices: Start specific with your first-party data, then refine. Test combinations of customer lists, custom segments, demographics and interests to see what performs best.

Advanced Optimization Strategies

Take Your Campaigns to the Next Level

Once your campaign is running, these advanced tactics will help maximize results:

Fine-Tune Your Feed

Update your Merchant Center feed regularly with keyword-rich titles and segmented products.

Use Negative Keywords

Block irrelevant searches to save budget. Check search term reports weekly.

Leverage Search Themes

Add keywords your customers search to steer the AI toward better performance.

Track Offline Conversions

For lead gen, import CRM data to optimize for real business outcomes.
